Hello! I'm Ady.

I'm an undergrad at UC Berkeley. I stumble around in CS, Philosophy, Economics & law school classes, professionally edging my major declaration. I'm on [leave](https://adymehta.substack.com/p/heres-to-the-gaps) to sidequest in vibrant communities, niche ideas, and meaningful impact for the world.

Currently, I research technical AI governance, in a subfield called "Compute Governance". [This spring](https://erafellowship.org/), I'm researching compliance verifiability from high memory bandwidth substrates of semiconductor chips for Saudi, South Korean, and Dutch geopolitical strategy.

In 2025, I proposed an [AI Dividend Fund](https://www.dailycal.org/news/campus/uc-institute-hosts-symposium-to-discuss-ai-policy/article_039b4c0b-cd15-4383-8903-4c20273154f1.html) and contributed to the [Remote Labor Index](https://www.remotelabor.ai/) after watching my parents lose their jobs to AI. Prior to AI safety, I supported friends building [AI for finance bros](https://www.docubridge.ai/), [polymers for Mars rover parachutes](https://conrad.spacecenter.org/challenge-alumni/meet-the-alc-board/), and [youth eating disorder legislation](https://hsph.harvard.edu/research/eating-disorders-striped/).

I'm a passionate community-builder. You can find me waffling in [paper reading groups](https://berkeleyaisafety.com/readinggroup) at home, at technical governance workshops in [Singapore](https://www.aisafety.sg/), and to [84 AI safety university groups](https://pathfinder.kairos-project.org/) across the world (via Zoom™)…or doing random ops work at [one](https://palisaderesearch.org/) [of](https://civai.org/) [these](https://bluedot.org/) [places](https://kairos-project.org/). I also enjoy hosting [hackathons](https://builddefenses.ai/), [rabbit-hole-a-thons](https://partiful.com/e/XW0CokIh05MvMExgMXmL), and [retreats](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/aditya-mehta-7892311a2_last-weekend-we-organized-ibar-the-intercollegiate-activity-7321214998842662913-P6aO/).

I grew up in three countries, champion the elaichi > ginger > saunf chai pipeline, and enjoy listening to 2002 John Mayer. My retirement dream is to be a kindergarten teacher. If you're up to play spikeball or chat about cricket, watch the sunset or go on a hike, or learn guitar or how to drive together, *hearing from you would make my day.*
